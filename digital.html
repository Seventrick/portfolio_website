The Lost Photographer

In my "Digital Imaging" class, we were given pretty much free reign to create whatever we wanted in Photoshop for our final. I decided to make a series of images which would go alongside a journal, telling the story of a photographer who awoke in a strange world with limit film and a journal. The teacher wanted these projects to be presented on a wall and therefore did not want the journal to be made. To this day I have the journal I was going to use and the story I was going to write, but a part of me likes the mystery of not exactly knowing this 'mysterious photographer's' story.
