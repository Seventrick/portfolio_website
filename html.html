Face Man

My first time taking "Internet Art", I created a choose your own adventure type game with a character I called Face Man. The experience is supposed to represent the feeling of loneliness from social anxiety while also being enjoyable. I wanted Face Man to act as a cute icon that the user wouldn't expect to be sad inside. This is one of my favorite projects I have done and plan to revisit the same idea in the future.

https://darienbateson.github.io/Face-Man/

Internet Art Portfolio

The second time I took "Internet Art", I made a portfolio page with all of the work I made for that class. I wanted to do it in the form of a game though, where the user has to find the 5 projects with a map and journal to guide them. While it is not as complete as I would have liked it to be, this project was also a huge success and I had a great time making it.
